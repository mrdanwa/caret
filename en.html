Who Are We?


Caret Capital is a firm specialized in real estate projects with the goal of maximizing capital returns. Our approach is based on active management of various types of projects (flipping, new construction, purchase options, NPLs, auctions, etc.) and the provision of strategic advisory services. Our young and dynamic team integrates cutting-edge algorithms and technologies at every stage of the process, allowing us to identify, filter, and structure only the best opportunities in the real estate market.

Our philosophy is based on three fundamental pillars: transparency, ethics, and excellence. We believe in the importance of managing each project with the utmost rigor, ensuring the alignment of interests and offering optimal solutions for all parties involved. Our commitment is to provide a comprehensive and differentiated service, guaranteeing our clients and collaborators a professional, efficient, and results-oriented approach.
